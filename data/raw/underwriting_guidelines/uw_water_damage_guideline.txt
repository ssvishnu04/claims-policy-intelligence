Underwriting Guideline: Water Damage Review

Water losses resulting from sudden and accidental discharge are generally eligible for coverage review under standard homeowners policies, subject to exclusions, deductible application, and policy conditions.

Claims involving repeated seepage, maintenance neglect, unresolved plumbing deterioration, or excluded water sources such as flood should be escalated for additional review.

Adjusters should verify:
- Cause of loss
- Duration of leakage
- Presence of flood indicators
- Prior related claims
- Policy effective dates
