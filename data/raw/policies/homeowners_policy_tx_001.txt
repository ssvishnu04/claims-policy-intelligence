Product: Homeowners HO-3
Insured State: Texas

SECTION I – PROPERTY COVERAGES
We cover direct physical loss to property described in Coverage A unless the loss is excluded under this policy.

WATER DAMAGE
We cover sudden and accidental discharge or overflow of water or steam from within a plumbing, heating, air conditioning, or automatic fire protective sprinkler system or from within a household appliance.

EXCLUSIONS
We do not cover loss caused directly or indirectly by:
1. Flood, surface water, waves, tidal water, overflow of a body of water, or spray from any of these.
2. Water below the surface of the ground, including water that exerts pressure on or seeps through a building.
3. Repeated or continuous seepage or leakage of water over a period of 14 days or more.
4. Neglect of the insured to use all reasonable means to save and preserve property after the time of loss.

DEDUCTIBLE
A deductible of $2,500 applies to each covered property loss.

LOSS SETTLEMENT
Covered property losses are settled at actual cash value at the time of loss, unless replacement cost coverage applies.
